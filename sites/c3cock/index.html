What this is

A pet cage worth looking at, built around the animal that lives in it.

C³COCK designs and builds modular cages for critters with strong opinions. Real wood, quiet hardware, full-size doors, and the kind of texture and lighting that keeps a small animal curious for more than a week.

01

Design

Layouts sized to your floor, your species, and your pet's stress level. We ask about climbing, burrowing, sun, and noise before we draw a line.

02

Build

Modular frames, real birch and oak, full-width doors, and silent running wheels. No plastic smell, no sharp edges, no mystery hardware.

03

Equip

UV-B lamps, basking spots, drip systems, climbing walls, feeding stations, and night-cycle lighting. Wired to one tidy panel.

04

Care

Refurbishment, replacement parts, and upgrades on demand. Send a photo of a chewed corner, get a panel back in the post.
