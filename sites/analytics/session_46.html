Submit a `Pixelfestsetzungsantrag` via https://pixeleb.be:

1. Go to the `pixelebbe!` assembly in the middle of the hackspace.
2. Call the voice bot via DECT: `74950` / `PIXL0`.
3. Send a handwritten Chaos postcard to `pixelebbe! GPN24 Pixel Office`.

You need to provide:

`x` coordinate, `y` coordinate, and color.

For the voice bot, use:

```text
#x#y#color*
```

Example: `#10#20#8*` sets pixel `(10,20)` to color ID `8` (`W4`, white).

Note: voice bot coordinates are `x 0-160`, `y 0-120`; postcard coordinates are `x 0-40`, `y 0-30`.
